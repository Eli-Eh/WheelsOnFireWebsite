About John O'Reilly

John O'Reilly is a car entheusiast, residing in Dublin, Ireland, who has his own magazine line, named "Wheels on Fire". He has a large collection of cars, which he writes reviews about, to help consumers make informed decisions about what cars they buy. He also commonly does car meets, and hosts events for car enthusiasts. His reviews, and a virtual look at his collection are avaliable here on the Wheels on Fire website, for your reading pleasure.

About Wheels on Fire

Wheels on Fire is a magazine line founded by John O'Rielly. It is a magazine detailing his collection, including reviews of the cars he owns. Wheels on Fire was originally founded in 1996, when John started getting frustrated with the amount of sponsorships present in car reviews. Wheels on Fire is known for John's witty writing and brutal honesty, which he still holds today.
